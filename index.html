Entertainment Media Retail

EMR is a business dealing with tailored audio visual installations of varying scales. From simple home theatre solutions or large multi room home entertainment solutions adapted for control using a user’s phone to audio, video, lights and sound proofing installations for auditoriums or complex automated multi-zone systems that could span an entire institute on multiple levels and control it’s schedules (e.g. school class times) and emergency procedures.

What We Do



Smart Home Installations

Enjoy a life without chaos, clutter, wires or missing remotes. Smarthome puts you back in control of all your home's devices and systems with a single button. Take complete control of your home with Smarthome automation solutions.



Evacuation System

EMR has been installing and servicing emergency evacuation systems in commercial buildings, schools, and businesses for many years. We work directly with architects and engineers to design a system that is custom tailored to assist in evacuating and maintaining order in emergency situations.



Auditorium Installations

Large-scale sound and video systems, like those found in churches and auditoriums, require a high level of design and installation skill. These systems usually have many requirements, often by multiple users. Our designers and installers can help you find and implement the best products for your environment



Structured Cabling

A properly designed and installed structured cabling system provides a cabling infrastructure that delivers predictable performance as well as has the flexibility to accommodate moves, adds and changes; maximizes system availability, provides redundancy; and future proofs the usability of the cabling system.



Home Entertainment

Easy to use multi-room solutions for indoor and outdoor entertainment;

  • Clatter free “Invisible” home audio & video solutions
  • Hidden all-weather speakers for outside use
  • Automation of home features like lights,doors and curtains
  • Ability for full control by mobile device

Top-to-bottom professional audio-visual solution including;
